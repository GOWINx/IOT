Predictive Analytics

Predictive Analytics is a forward-looking approach that combines historical data, statistical models, and machine learning algorithms to predict future events. It helps organizations answer the critical question: “What is likely to happen next?”

Predictive models are trained using a large volume of historical data. These models learn patterns and apply statistical techniques to generate forecasts. For example, a company can predict product demand next month based on previous sales trends.

Dashboards provide intuitive visuals that help decision-makers explore forecasts. Users can filter results, monitor anomalies, and take pre-emptive actions based on model suggestions. This is used widely in finance, health, e-commerce, and cybersecurity.
